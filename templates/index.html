Where it all began

The origins of the term barbecue are unclear but the general concensus is that the 'Spanish' first used the term 'Barbacoa' to refer to the natives of the 'Caribbean' cooking meat over a fire on a wooden frame.
The history of barbecuing meanwhile has a somewhat checkered past from cannibalism to slavery, but that's a story for another time!
The social event that we know as barbecuing today started in the 1940's after the end of the second world war and evolved quickly with the invention of the gas barbecue coming along in the 1950's. Now a common feature in many gardens they are a great excuse for people to get together over a beer and a burger.
